AC Vent Monitor

Temperature and humidity data of multiple AC vents in my house, collected in online data feeds and visualized in p5js.

Central air conditioning provides temperature readings of the entire home, but only an estimate based on an average value if not a single sample. This isn't true to reality where different vents can have varying degrees of effectiveness both heating and cooling the house. What I wanted to measure was how strong individual AC vents in my home were, or at least comparing what I felt to be the strongest and weakest vents. The measurement process was simple: place a temperature and humidity sensor point blank against a vent and upload the readings to a feed to see if there was a noticeable difference in temperature on hot and cool air coniditioning modes.
